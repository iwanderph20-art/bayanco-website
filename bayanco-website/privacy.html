Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and respect your privacy

Last Updated: February 2024

Privacy Policy

BayanCo ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process personal information in connection with our website, mobile application, and related services (the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using BayanCo, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all provisions of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our data practices, please do not use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture, and username
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued ID, tax identification number, and other verification documents
  • Address Information: Billing address, shipping address, and location data

Campaign Information

  • Campaign title, description, and category
  • Campaign images and media files
  • Campaign timeline and updates
  • Funding goals and actual amounts raised

Payment Information

  • Payment details (processed securely through PayMongo and PayMaya)
  • GCash account information (when using GCash payment option)
  • Payment transaction history

Usage Data

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on Service
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Search queries and interactions with campaigns

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Processing campaign creation and funding
  • Facilitating transactions between backers and campaign creators
  • Providing customer support

Verification & Compliance

  • Verifying user identity and preventing fraud
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Conducting Know Your Customer (KYC) checks
  • Anti-money laundering (AML) monitoring

Communication

  • Sending transactional emails (confirmations, receipts, updates)
  • Marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Platform notifications and important announcements
  • Customer service inquiries and support

Security & Safety

  • Monitoring for fraudulent activity
  • Protecting against unauthorized access
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Investigating and preventing illegal activities

Analytics & Improvement

  • Analyzing platform usage patterns
  • Improving user experience and features
  • Aggregating statistical information
  • Conducting research and surveys

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with third parties only in the following circumstances:

Payment Processors

We share payment information with third-party payment processors (PayMongo and PayMaya, both with GCash options) to process transactions. These processors are BSP-licensed and comply with PCI DSS standards.

Verification Services

We may share information with identity verification and KYC service providers to confirm user identity and prevent fraud.

Legal Compliance

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, government agency request, or to protect our legal rights and the safety of our users.

Business Partners

Campaign creators' public profile information may be shared with backers and displayed on the platform. This includes name, profile picture, and campaign information.

Service Providers

We may share information with third parties who provide services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, customer support) under confidentiality agreements.

Business Transfers

If BayanCo is involved in a merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

With Your Consent

We will not share your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

Encryption

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all platform interactions

Secure Storage

  • Data stored on secure, firewalled servers
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Restricted physical access to data centers

Access Controls

  • Authentication mechanisms (passwords, two-factor authentication)
  • Role-based access control for employees
  • Monitoring and logging of data access

Incident Response

  • Incident detection and response procedures
  • Notification requirements in case of data breach
  • Regular security training for employees

Note: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

You have the right to request and receive a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

Right to Correction

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.

Right to Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., legal hold, active campaigns).

Right to Data Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your data in a portable format that can be transferred to another service.

Right to Object

You may object to certain uses of your information, particularly for marketing communications.

Right to Withdraw Consent

If we process your information based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@bayanco.org with your request and proof of identity.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

Essential Cookies

  • Session cookies for account authentication
  • CSRF protection tokens
  • Platform functionality cookies

Analytics Cookies

  • Google Analytics for usage statistics
  • Heat mapping tools for user experience optimization

Cookie Management

You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect platform functionality.

Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals, though our response depends on applicable law.

7. Children's Privacy

BayanCo is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will delete their account and associated information.

If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately at privacy@bayanco.org.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Account Information: Retained while account is active and for 2 years after deletion
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years (required by law)
  • KYC/Verification Documents: Retained for 5 years
  • Campaign Data: Retained for the life of the campaign plus 2 years
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you opt-out

We may retain certain information longer if required by law or if needed for legitimate business purposes.

9. International Data Transfers

BayanCo is Philippines-based and data is primarily stored in the Philippines. However:

  • We may transfer data to other countries for processing, backup, or service provision
  • If you access BayanCo from outside the Philippines, your data may be transferred internationally
  • We implement appropriate safeguards for international transfers (Data Processing Agreements, Standard Contractual Clauses)

By using BayanCo, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification if the changes are material
  • Requiring your consent for material changes that affect how we use your existing information

Your continued use of the Service after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
