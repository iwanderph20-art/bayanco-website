About BayanCo

Empowering communities through collaborative funding

Our Mission

At BayanCo, we believe in the power of community. Our mission is to democratize funding for meaningful projects across the Philippines, enabling anyone with a great idea to transform their vision into reality. We embody the spirit of bayanihan—working together for the common good.

Core Values

Bayanihan

Community unity drives everything we do. We believe in the collective strength of neighbors helping neighbors.

Transparency

We are open and honest about how funds are used. Every transaction is traceable and every update is genuine.

Trust

Verified creators, secure payments, and protected supporters. Your trust is our most valuable asset.

Our Story

BayanCo was born from a simple observation: some of the most innovative and meaningful projects in the Philippines never get funded because they fall outside traditional banking channels. A local baker in Davao with a brilliant idea, a teacher in Iloilo developing educational technology, a community artist in Mindanao—they all had the vision and capability, but lacked the resources.

In 2023, Elle and Eunice Borja saw an opportunity to change this. They recognized that the spirit of bayanihan—the Filipino tradition of communal unity and cooperation—was the missing ingredient. What if we could harness the power of community to fund community projects?

What started as a conversation over coffee became a mission. BayanCo was launched with a singular focus: to make crowdfunding accessible, transparent, and rooted in community values. We've grown from processing our first campaign in a small office to supporting thousands of projects across the archipelago.

Today, BayanCo stands as a testament to the power of collective action. Every campaign funded, every dream realized, and every community strengthened reinforces our belief that together, we can build a better future for all Filipinos.
