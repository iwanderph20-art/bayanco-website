Fraud Prevention

We actively detect and prevent fraud using advanced monitoring systems and manual review. Here's what we watch for:

💰 Suspiciously Large Goals

We review campaigns with goals significantly higher than comparable projects in the same category.

🔄 Duplicate Accounts

Our system detects multiple accounts from the same person attempting to create artificial campaigns or pledges.

⚠️ Vague Project Descriptions

Campaigns lacking detail, realistic timelines, or clear funding allocation are flagged for additional review.

📸 Low-Quality Media

Campaigns with no photos, AI-generated images, or heavily edited content receive extra scrutiny.

🚩 Rapid Account Activity

New accounts attempting to launch campaigns immediately or making large pledges in short time periods are monitored.

🗺️ Location Inconsistencies

We verify creator location matches project location and check for suspicious geographic patterns.

📞 Contact Verification Failure

Creators unable to verify phone or email, or with false contact information, are blocked from fundraising.

💳 Payment Pattern Anomalies

Unusual payment patterns or funding sources are flagged by our fraud detection algorithms.

🔗 Similar Campaign Patterns

We detect and investigate campaigns with suspiciously similar descriptions, goals, or content.

⭐ Fake Review Detection

We monitor for artificially inflated ratings, reviews, and endorsements using linguistic analysis.
