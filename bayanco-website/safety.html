Safety & Trust

We take trust seriously. BayanCo combines verification, automated risk checks, community reporting, and human review to protect donors and campaigners.

Verification Levels

✓ Basic Phone + Email

Instant verification - Quick and easy to get started. Suitable for smaller campaigns from trusted community members.

✓✓ Enhanced Government ID

24-hour review - We verify your identity with government-issued ID. Builds stronger trust for medium-sized campaigns.

✓✓✓ Verified Barangay Clearance

3-5 day review - The highest level of verification includes barangay clearance. Recommended for large campaigns and maximum donor confidence.

Community Endorsements

(Optional but recommended)

Campaigners can request endorsements from:

  • Barangay captain
  • Church/religious leader
  • 3+ neighbors

Endorsers receive a link via SMS/email to vouch for the campaign, adding an extra layer of community trust.

Fraud Prevention

We automatically flag risk signals such as:

  • High goals without ID verification
  • Copied campaign descriptions
  • Suspicious image sources
  • Unusual campaign creation patterns
  • Suspicious donation spikes

Manual review triggers include flagged campaigns, multiple user reports, and very large requested amounts.

Community Reporting

Every campaign can be reported for fraud, inappropriate content, scams, or fake identity. Multiple reports can auto-pause a campaign until reviewed.

Our trust and safety team investigates all reports within 24 hours to protect our community.
