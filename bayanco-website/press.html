Press & Media

Learn about BayanCo's impact on Filipino communities worldwide

Story Angles

  • Empowering Barangay Communities BayanCo brings grassroots crowdfunding directly to Filipino communities, enabling local projects and entrepreneurs to access capital without traditional gatekeepers.
  • Trust at Scale Our multi-tiered verification system and community endorsements create transparency while protecting both campaign creators and donors from fraud.
  • Financial Inclusion for OFWs Filipino overseas workers can support their hometowns and families directly through meaningful investment in community-driven initiatives.
  • Real Social Impact Metrics Unlike traditional crowdfunding, BayanCo tracks and validates real-world outcomes of funded campaigns, demonstrating tangible community benefits.
  • Digital Transformation in Rural Philippines BayanCo bridges the digital divide, bringing modern financial technology to barangays and municipalities previously underserved by fintech solutions.
