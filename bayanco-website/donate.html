⚠ Important: How Donations Work on BayanCo

BayanCo does not hold, manage, or process any funds. When you donate, your payment goes directly to the campaign creator through their chosen payment method (PayMongo or PayMaya via GCash). BayanCo is a platform that connects donors with campaign creators only.

Donations are non-refundable once sent. Please review the campaign details and creator profile carefully before donating. If you suspect fraud, report it here.

Donor Safety Tips

  • Review the campaign description and creator profile carefully before donating
  • Look for verified campaigns with the verification badge
  • Check campaign updates — active creators who post updates are more trustworthy
  • Be cautious of campaigns with unrealistic goals or vague descriptions
  • Only donate amounts you are comfortable with and can afford
  • Report suspicious campaigns immediately through our Contact page
