Tips for Campaign Creators

How to Set Up & Raise Money on BayanCo

A step-by-step guide to launching your successful fundraising campaign

Step 1: Create Your Campaign

1
Choose a Compelling Title - Make it clear and attention-grabbing
2
Write a Clear Description - Explain your cause and why you need help
3
Upload High-Quality Photos - Use clear, authentic images that tell your story

Step 2: Set Your Goal

1
Be Realistic - Set a funding target that matches your actual needs
2
Explain Usage - Clearly state how funds will be used
3
Break It Down - Show the specific breakdown of expenses

Step 3: Get Verified

1
Phone & Email Verification - Instant verification to establish trust
2
Government ID Upload - Enhance credibility with official identification
3
Barangay Endorsement - Get maximum credibility with local support

Step 4: Share Your Campaign

1
Social Media - Share on Facebook, Messenger, Viber groups
2
Personal Network - Reach out to family, friends, and community
3
OFW Networks - Overseas Filipino workers are powerful allies

Step 5: Keep Donors Updated

1
Regular Updates - Post updates with photos and receipts
2
Show Impact - Demonstrate exactly where money goes
3
Express Gratitude - Thank your donors publicly and privately

Step 6: Fees & Payments

4% Platform Fee Supporting our mission to help communities
Payment Processing (PayMongo or PayMaya via GCash) 2.5% + ₱10 per transaction
Total Fees: ~6.5-7% Lower than most platforms!
