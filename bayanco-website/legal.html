Important Information for Donors & Fee Structure

Last Updated: February 02, 2026

IMPORTANT INFORMATION FOR DONORS

This is the official disclaimer of Bayanco.org. Please read it carefully before using our platform or making any donations.

About Bayanco

Bayanco is a crowdfunding platform operating from the Philippines. We provide technology that connects generous donors with individuals and causes in need, with a special focus on Filipino communities worldwide. We are dedicated to creating a transparent, trustworthy platform where Filipinos help Filipinos.

Our Platform Model

Bayanco is a technology service, not a charitable organization. We provide the platform infrastructure; campaign creators are independent users who run their own fundraising campaigns.

When you donate:

  • Your contribution goes directly to the campaign creator you select
  • Payments are processed by PayMongo and PayMaya, BSP-licensed payment service providers
  • Bayanco facilitates the connection but does not act as a charity or fiscal sponsor

Tax Deductibility

Donations made through Bayanco are NOT tax-deductible as charitable contributions. Campaign creators are typically individuals, not BIR-recognized charitable organizations. We recommend consulting a tax professional about reporting your contributions.

While your donation may not provide a tax benefit, your generosity creates real, meaningful impact in people's lives.

Due Diligence & Platform Safety

We want your giving experience to be safe and positive:

  • ✓ We use industry-standard security and encryption
  • ✓ Payments are processed by regulated financial institutions
  • ✓ We review campaigns for compliance with our policies
  • ✓ We provide tools to report suspicious activity
  • ✓ We investigate fraud reports and take action when warranted

However, we cannot guarantee:

  • How recipients use donated funds
  • The accuracy of all campaign information
  • Specific outcomes or results

Campaign creators are solely responsible for their campaigns and fund usage.

We encourage you to:

  • Read campaign descriptions carefully
  • Research causes that matter to you
  • Ask campaign creators questions
  • Start with smaller donations if unsure

Fees & Transparency

Bayanco charges a platform fee of 4% to maintain and improve our services. Payment processing fees vary by payment method:

  • GCash (via PayMongo): 2.5% + ₱10
  • GCash (via PayMaya): 2.5% + ₱10

All fees are clearly shown before you complete your donation.

Your Privacy

We protect your information using industry-standard security practices. We never sell your personal data. Payment details are handled by our secure payment processors and are not stored on our servers. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Refunds & Disputes

Donations are generally final once processed. If you believe a campaign is fraudulent or have a serious concern, contact us immediately at support@bayanco.org. We'll investigate and work with you to resolve legitimate issues, though we cannot guarantee refunds.

Governing Law & Disputes

This platform is operated from the Philippines. Use of Bayanco is governed by Philippine law. By using our platform, you agree to our Terms of Service.

Your Impact Matters

Despite the legal formalities, every donation makes a real difference. Thank you for your generosity, trust, and commitment to helping others. Together, we're making the world a little better.
