Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BayanCo

For Campaign Creators

  • BayanCo charges a 4% platform fee on funds successfully raised. There are no setup fees, monthly fees, or upfront costs. If your campaign raises ₱0, you pay ₱0 in platform fees. Payment processing is handled by PayMongo at 2.5% + ₱10 per GCash transaction. Total fees are approximately 6.5-7%, which is 1-3% lower than most competitors. Donors may optionally add a 10% tip to support the platform (fully adjustable or removable).
  • Campaign duration can range from 7 to 90 days. You choose the timeline when creating your campaign. Most campaigns run for 30-45 days to maintain momentum and donor engagement.
  • Share detailed project descriptions, high-quality photos and videos, your personal story, and realistic timelines. Complete verification steps (phone, email, or government ID) to increase credibility. Regular updates and transparent communication also build strong donor relationships.
  • BayanCo does not hold your funds. When a donor contributes to your campaign, payment is processed directly to you through PayMongo or PayMaya (via GCash). There is no withdrawal step — you receive donations as they come in. BayanCo only facilitates the connection between donors and campaign creators.
  • You still keep all donations received. Since BayanCo does not hold funds — payments go directly from donors to you via PayMongo or PayMaya (GCash) — there are no refunds and no "all-or-nothing" requirement. Whatever amount donors have contributed is already yours. You're welcome to continue promoting your campaign or create a new one anytime.
  • Yes, regular updates are strongly encouraged and expected. We recommend posting updates at least monthly during your campaign and after it ends. Updates help maintain donor trust, show project progress, and increase likelihood of campaign success. Lack of updates may result in campaign removal.

For Donors

  • We currently accept payments via PayMongo and PayMaya, both of which offer GCash as a payment option. All transactions are encrypted and processed securely through BSP-licensed payment service providers.
  • Yes, you can choose to donate anonymously when making your pledge. Your name will not be displayed on the campaign page, but you'll still receive updates and receipts. Campaign creators will not see your personal information if you choose this option.
  • Yes, you'll automatically receive an email receipt for your donation. You can also download receipts from your donor dashboard anytime. Please note that donations made through BayanCo are NOT tax-deductible as charitable contributions, as campaign creators are typically individuals, not BIR-recognized charitable organizations.
  • If a campaign doesn't reach its funding goal, your donation is automatically refunded to your original payment method within 7-10 business days. No action is needed on your part. You'll receive a notification when the refund is processed.
  • Use the "Report" button on any campaign page or contact our support team at support@bayanco.org. Provide details about your concern, and our team will investigate. We take all fraud reports seriously and may suspend campaigns or remove creators found to be fraudulent.

Safety & Trust

  • We use AI-powered fraud detection, manual review of campaigns, and multi-tier verification systems. We monitor for suspicious patterns, verify creator identity and location, check for duplicate accounts, and maintain a community reporting system. We also work with authorities to investigate serious fraud.
  • Basic verification requires phone and email confirmation (instant). Enhanced verification requires government-issued ID verification (24 hours). Verified status requires a barangay clearance or community endorsement (3-5 days). Higher verification levels increase campaign credibility and donor confidence.
  • Community endorsements are recommendations from local leaders (barangay officials, community organizations) confirming a creator's legitimacy and project value. This provides third-party validation and significantly boosts campaign credibility, especially in rural communities where trust networks are strong.
  • We use bank-level encryption for all data transmission, comply with Philippine data protection laws, and never share personal information with third parties without consent. We regularly conduct security audits and employ professional security teams. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
