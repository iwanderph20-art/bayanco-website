Our Story

BayanCo was born from a simple observation: Filipinos are among the most generous people in the world, always ready to help family, friends, and even strangers in need. Yet existing crowdfunding platforms often didn't serve our community well‚Äîwith high fees, lack of GCash integration, and limited transparency requirements that left donors uncertain about how their money was used.

Eunice and Elle Borja, Filipino women who experienced the power of community support firsthand, decided to create something better. They envisioned a platform that would combine modern technology with traditional Filipino values of bayanihan.

"We wanted to build more than just a platform‚Äîwe wanted to create a movement where every Filipino, wherever they are in the world, could participate in helping our kababayans achieve their dreams."

What makes BayanCo different is our unwavering commitment to transparency. Every campaign creator must provide regular updates with photos and receipts. Donors can see exactly how their money is being used. It's accountability built into the platform, not just promised in fine print.

We're proud to be a Filipino-owned and operated platform, based in the Philippines, serving Filipino communities worldwide. We process all payments through PayMongo‚Äîa trusted Philippine payment gateway supporting GCash, GrabPay, credit/debit cards, and over-the-counter options‚Äîcharge only 3-6% in platform fees (lower than most competitors), and require genuine transparency from every campaign creator.

Today, BayanCo is growing as more and more Filipinos discover a crowdfunding platform that truly understands and serves our community. Every successful campaign, every peso raised, every life changed‚Äîit's all a testament to the enduring power of the Filipino spirit of helping one another.
