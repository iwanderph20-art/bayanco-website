Why Choose BayanCo?

Built by Filipinos, for Filipinos—with features that matter to our community

Filipino-First

Designed specifically for Filipino communities with PayMongo—supporting GCash, credit/debit cards, e-wallets, and over-the-counter payments.

Full Transparency

Every campaign shows receipts, photos, and regular updates. See exactly where your money goes.

Verified Trust

Three-tier verification system with optional community endorsements from barangay leaders.

Lower Fees

Only 3-6% platform fees—keeping more money in Filipino communities where it belongs.

Fast Disbursement

Quick withdrawal options including GCash, bank transfer, and remittance partners.

Disaster Ready

Rapid response campaigns for typhoons, earthquakes, and calamities affecting our communities.
